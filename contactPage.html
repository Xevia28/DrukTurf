Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

Our support team typically responds within 1-2 business hours during our operational hours. For urgent matters, we recommend using our live chat feature for immediate assistance.

Can I make a booking over the phone?

Yes! You can call our booking hotline during business hours to make reservations. However, we recommend using our online platform for convenience and real-time availability.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours prior to booking are eligible for full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours of the booking time are subject to a 50% fee. Please see our terms for details.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special discounts for group bookings of 10+ people. Contact our corporate team at corporate@drukturf.com to discuss special rates for your group or organization.
